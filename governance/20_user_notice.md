# 20 — User Notice

## Notice shown to users

You are interacting with an automated HR policy assistant. It provides general information from approved policies and does not make employment decisions.

Do not enter names, email addresses, phone numbers, employee IDs, salary information, medical information, performance information, complaints, or disciplinary information.

The assistant:

- does not access individual employee records;
- does not approve requests;
- does not provide legal or medical advice;
- does not rank or evaluate employees;
- may refer you to Human Resources;
- does not intentionally retain your raw question in the audit log.

For individual, sensitive, disputed, or legal matters, contact Human Resources through the approved confidential channel.

## Future real deployment

A real deployment must identify the organization, privacy contact, lawful processing basis, rights process, retention period, relevant processors, international transfers, grievance contact, and applicable jurisdiction-specific information.
