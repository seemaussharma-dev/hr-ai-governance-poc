# 01 — Project Charter

## Project name

HR AI Governance POC — Stage 1

## Sponsor

Fictional HR Director, ABC Horizon Media

## Product owner

AI Governance and Technology Lead

## Purpose

Create a public-safe proof of concept that demonstrates practical AI governance for an internal HR policy assistant.

## Objectives

- Reduce repetitive HR policy questions in a future pilot.
- Provide consistent answers from approved documents.
- Prevent personal-data processing in the public POC.
- Prevent automated employment decisions.
- Demonstrate regulatory and management-system controls.
- Create reusable evidence for interviews and portfolio review.

## In scope

- Synthetic leave, remote-work, and expense policies.
- General employee policy questions.
- Local policy retrieval.
- Input guardrails.
- Source citations.
- Human escalation.
- Minimal audit evidence.
- Automated tests.
- Governance documentation.

## Out of scope

- Real employee data.
- Payroll, benefits, medical, performance, complaint, or disciplinary systems.
- Candidate screening.
- Employee scoring or profiling.
- Hiring, promotion, discipline, or termination decisions.
- Legal advice.
- Claude API integration.
- Production authentication and cloud deployment.

## Deliverables

- Working application.
- Synthetic policy set.
- Automated tests.
- Governance documentation.
- Regulatory control matrix.
- Risk register.
- Traceability matrix.
- Demo script.
- Stage 2 roadmap.

## Constraints

- Free local tools.
- No API key.
- No real personal data.
- Public GitHub suitability.

## Approval gates

1. Business and scope approval.
2. Privacy and risk review.
3. Test completion.
4. GitHub publication review.
5. Stage 2 approval before Claude integration.
