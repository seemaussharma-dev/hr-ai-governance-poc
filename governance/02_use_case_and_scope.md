# 02 — Use Case and Scope

## Intended use

Provide general information from approved synthetic HR policies covering leave, remote work, and expense reimbursement.

## Intended users

Employees and managers seeking general policy information.

## Allowed examples

- “How many vacation days do employees receive?”
- “How do I request remote work?”
- “When must an expense report be submitted?”

## Prohibited uses

The system must not:

- retrieve or infer an individual employee record;
- disclose salary, payroll, medical, disability, performance, complaint, investigation, or disciplinary information;
- rank candidates or employees;
- recommend hiring, promotion, discipline, or termination;
- predict resignation or employee behavior;
- provide legal or medical advice;
- process real personal data in the public POC.

## Decision authority

Informational only. The system has no authority to approve leave, determine eligibility, authorize remote work, approve payment, or make an employment decision.

## Human oversight

HR owns policy interpretation, exceptions, disputes, individual circumstances, and sensitive matters.

## Risk classification decision

This Stage 1 prototype is intentionally designed as a limited informational policy assistant. It is not intended for recruitment, worker management, employee evaluation, or access to employment, which would require a fresh EU AI Act classification and stronger controls.

## Scope-change triggers

Reassessment is mandatory before adding:

- Claude or another external model;
- real employee questions or records;
- user authentication;
- HR-system integration;
- personalized answers;
- candidate or employee evaluation;
- automated workflow actions;
- cloud hosting;
- additional countries or legal jurisdictions.
