# 22 — Supplier Assessment for Stage 2 Claude Integration

Stage 1 has no external AI processor. Before Claude API integration, assess:

## Commercial and service

- API account and billing model.
- Rate limits and service availability.
- Support and incident contacts.
- Exit and portability.

## Privacy

- Roles of organization and provider.
- Input/output retention.
- Training-use terms.
- Data location.
- International transfers and safeguards.
- Subprocessors.
- Data deletion.
- Data-subject request support.
- Contract and data-processing terms.

## Security

- Encryption.
- Access control.
- API-key protection.
- Certifications and assurance reports.
- Vulnerability and incident processes.
- Tenant isolation.
- Logging.
- Abuse monitoring.

## Model governance

- Exact model/version.
- Model changes and deprecation.
- Safety documentation.
- Known limitations.
- Output ownership and intellectual-property terms.
- Evaluation and red-team expectations.

## Approval condition

Do not send real HR content or employee questions until privacy, security, legal, HR, and AI governance owners approve the supplier and the new architecture.
