## Annual Vacation Entitlement
Full-time employees receive 15 paid vacation days in each calendar year. Part-time employees receive a prorated entitlement based on scheduled hours. These values are fictional and are not connected to any real employee record.

## Request and Approval
Employees should submit a vacation request to their manager at least 10 calendar days before the planned absence. The manager reviews operational coverage and normally responds within three business days. A request is not approved until the manager confirms approval.

## Carryover
Employees may carry over up to five unused vacation days into the next calendar year. Carried-over vacation should be used by March 31 unless Human Resources approves an exception.

## Human Support
Questions about an individual balance, eligibility, legal entitlement, or special circumstance must be handled confidentially by Human Resources.
