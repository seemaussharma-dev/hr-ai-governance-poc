## Policy Statement

ssp-dev-hr-aigovern-poc is committed to providing equal employment
opportunity to qualified applicants and employees.

Employment decisions must be based on legitimate, job-related requirements
and business needs.

No applicant or employee will be discriminated against because of race,
color, religion, sex, pregnancy, sexual orientation, transgender status,
national origin, age, disability, genetic information, or any other
characteristic protected by applicable law.

## Scope

This policy applies to applicants and employees.

It applies to recruitment, hiring, work assignments, compensation, training,
promotion, performance management, discipline, termination, and other
employment activities.

## Equal and Objective Treatment

Employment criteria must be relevant to the position and applied consistently.

Protected characteristics must not be used to rank, score, select, reject,
promote, discipline, or terminate an applicant or employee.

## Anti-Retaliation

Retaliation against an individual who raises a good-faith discrimination
concern, participates in an authorized investigation, or exercises a
protected employment right is prohibited.

## Reporting Concerns

Applicants and employees may report equal-employment or discrimination
concerns to Human Resources through the approved confidential reporting
channel.

The HR Policy Assistant provides general policy information only.

It cannot determine whether unlawful discrimination occurred, investigate
an allegation, provide legal advice, or decide the outcome of a complaint.

## Human Decision Requirement

Hiring, promotion, discipline, termination, accommodation, complaint, and
investigation decisions must remain with authorized human reviewers.

The HR Policy Assistant must not make or recommend these decisions.
